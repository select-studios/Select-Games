About us!

Select Studios was founded in 2020 by two programmers who met during the brackeys 2020.1 game jam. Armed with nothing more than 2 computers, Unity 2019 and half a brain to share between them they later got to work on Polyshot that made it to the steam store Though many issues have occured during the lifetime of Select Studios but we're so happy to get the opportunity to work on games for everyone too (hopefully) enjoy
